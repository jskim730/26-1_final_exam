## Problem 1

**가정.** 각 $f^m_\theta$ 는 $C^1$ diffeomorphism → $f_\theta$ 도 $C^1$ diffeomorphism, Jacobian nonsingular.

### (a)

$f_\theta$ 가 bijective이므로 임의의 measurable set $A$ 에 대해

$$
P(X\in A) = P\big(Z_0\in f_\theta^{-1}(A)\big) = \int_{f_\theta^{-1}(A)} p_{Z_0}(z_0)\,dz_0 .
$$

$g_\theta:=f_\theta^{-1}$ 를 linearize하면 $g_\theta(x+\Delta x)\approx g_\theta(x)+J_{g_\theta}(x)\Delta x$ 이므로 $\Delta z\approx J_{g_\theta}(x)\Delta x$, 부피는 $|\det|$ 배 변환되어

$$
dz = \left|\det\frac{\partial f_\theta^{-1}(x)}{\partial x}\right|dx .
$$

대입하면 ($z_0\in f_\theta^{-1}(A)\iff x\in A$)

$$
P(X\in A)=\int_A p_{Z_0}\!\big(f_\theta^{-1}(x)\big)\left|\det\frac{\partial f_\theta^{-1}(x)}{\partial x}\right|dx .
$$

$A$ 가 임의이므로 integrand 일치:

$$
\boxed{\,p_X(x;\theta) = p_{Z_0}\!\big(f_\theta^{-1}(x)\big)\left|\det\frac{\partial f_\theta^{-1}(x)}{\partial x}\right| .}
$$

### (b)

Inverse는 역순 composition이므로 $z_{m-1}=(f^m_\theta)^{-1}(z_m),\ m=M,\dots,1$ ($z_M=x$). chain rule과 $\det(AB)=\det A\det B$ 로

$$
\left|\det\frac{\partial f_\theta^{-1}(x)}{\partial x}\right| = \prod_{m=1}^M\left|\det\frac{\partial (f^m_\theta)^{-1}(z_m)}{\partial z_m}\right|.
$$

(a)에 대입 ($p_{Z_0}(f_\theta^{-1}(x))=p_{Z_0}(z_0)$) → $p_X(x;\theta)=p_{Z_0}(z_0)\prod_m|\det\partial(f^m_\theta)^{-1}/\partial z_m|$. i.i.d. dataset에 대해 $L_{\mathrm{NLL}}=-\sum_i\log p_X(x^{(i)};\theta)$ 에 log를 취하면 ($z_M^{(i)}=x^{(i)},\ z_{m-1}^{(i)}=(f^m_\theta)^{-1}(z_m^{(i)})$)

$$
\boxed{\,L_{\mathrm{NLL}}(\theta) = -\sum_{i=1}^N\left[\log p_{Z_0}\!\big(z_0^{(i)}\big) + \sum_{m=1}^M\log\left|\det\frac{\partial (f^m_\theta)^{-1}(z_m^{(i)})}{\partial z_m^{(i)}}\right|\right].}
$$

## Problem 2

**Sensitivity.** $S_\theta(t):=\partial x_\theta(t)/\partial\theta$. state ODE를 $\theta$ 로 미분 (순서 교환) → $\dot S_\theta = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}S_\theta + \frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta}$.

### (a)

$J=\ell(x_\theta(t_1))$ 이므로 chain rule + $a_\theta(t_1)=\nabla_x\ell$:

$$
\nabla_\theta J(\theta) = S_\theta(t_1)^\top a_\theta(t_1). \tag{$\ast$}
$$

$S_\theta^\top a_\theta$ 를 미분하고 sensitivity ODE와 주어진 adjoint ODE $\dot a_\theta=-(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x})^\top a_\theta$ 를 대입하면 ($\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$ 항 상쇄)

$$
\frac{d}{dt}\big(S_\theta^\top a_\theta\big) = \Big(\tfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}S_\theta+\tfrac{\partial f}{\partial\theta}\Big)^\top a_\theta - S_\theta^\top\big(\tfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}\big)^\top a_\theta = \Big(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta}\Big)^\top a_\theta .
$$

$t_0\to t_1$ 적분: $S_\theta(t_1)^\top a_\theta(t_1) - S_\theta(t_0)^\top a_\theta(t_0) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta})^\top a_\theta\,dt$. $(\ast)$ 와 $S_\theta(t_0)=\partial x_0(\theta)/\partial\theta$ 대입:

$$
\boxed{\,\nabla_\theta J(\theta) = \Big(\frac{\partial x_0(\theta)}{\partial\theta}\Big)^\top a_\theta(t_0) + \int_{t_0}^{t_1}\Big(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta}\Big)^\top a_\theta(t)\,dt .}
$$

$x_0$ 가 $\theta$-independent면 첫 항 $=0$ → $\nabla_\theta J=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta})^\top a_\theta\,dt$.

### (b)

$\theta^{(k)}$ 에서: **Forward** $x_\theta(t_1)=\mathrm{ODESolve}(f, x_0(\theta^{(k)}), t_0, t_1)$, $a(t_1)=\nabla_x\ell(x_\theta(t_1))$. **Backward** ($t_1\to t_0$) augmented:

$$
\begin{bmatrix} x(t_0)\\ a(t_0)\\ g(t_0)\end{bmatrix}
=\mathrm{ODESolve}\!\left(\begin{bmatrix} f\\ -(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x})^\top a\\ -(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta})^\top a\end{bmatrix},\begin{bmatrix} x(t_1)\\ a(t_1)\\ \mathbf{0}_{|\theta|}\end{bmatrix}, t_1, t_0\right),
$$

$g(t_0)=\int_{t_0}^{t_1}(\frac{\partial f}{\partial\theta})^\top a\,dt$. **Gradient** $\nabla_\theta J=(\frac{\partial x_0}{\partial\theta})^\top a(t_0)+g(t_0)$, **update**:

$$
\boxed{\,\theta^{(k+1)}=\theta^{(k)}-\eta\,\nabla_\theta J(\theta^{(k)}).}
$$

## Problem 3

Forward $q_\phi$ = inference, reverse $p_\theta$ = generative.

**Markov & factorization.** Markov property $q_\phi(x_t\mid x_{0:t-1})=q_\phi(x_t\mid x_{t-1})$ + chain rule:

$$
q_\phi(x_{0:T})=q(x_0)\prod_{t=1}^T q_\phi(x_t\mid x_{t-1}),\qquad p_\theta(x_{0:T})=p_\theta(x_T)\prod_{t=1}^T p_\theta(x_{t-1}\mid x_t).
$$

고차원 joint가 local transition들의 product로 분해되어 tractable.

**Forward.** fixed Markov chain $q_\phi(x_t\mid x_{t-1})=\mathcal{N}(\sqrt{\alpha_t}x_{t-1},(1-\alpha_t)I)$, $\alpha_t=1-\beta_t$. schedule은 $q_\phi(x_T)\sim\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ 이 되도록 고정.

**Forward → Gaussian (Property #1).** reparameterization $x_t=\sqrt{\alpha_t}x_{t-1}+\sqrt{1-\alpha_t}\varepsilon_t$ 를 recursive하게 대입하면 $x_t=\sqrt{\bar\alpha_t}x_0+\sum_{s=1}^t c_{s,t}\varepsilon_s$ ($\bar\alpha_t=\prod_{s=1}^t\alpha_s$, $c_{s,t}=\sqrt{(1-\alpha_s)\prod_{i=s+1}^t\alpha_i}$). 독립 Gaussian 합이고 telescoping $\sum_s c_{s,t}^2=1-\bar\alpha_t$ 이므로

$$
q(x_t\mid x_0)=\mathcal{N}(\sqrt{\bar\alpha_t}\,x_0,(1-\bar\alpha_t)I).
$$

$\bar\alpha_T\to0$ 이면 $q(x_T\mid x_0)\to\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ ($x_0$ 무관). 즉 임의의 $q(x_0)$ 를 standard Gaussian으로 transport.

**Reverse.** $p_\theta(x_T)=\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ 에서 시작하는 learned Markov chain $p_\theta(x_{t-1}\mid x_t)=\mathcal{N}(\mu_\theta(x_t,t),\Sigma_\theta(x_t,t))$. 참 $q(x_{t-1}\mid x_t)$ 는 $q(x_0)$ marginalization을 요구해 intractable하나 $\beta_t$ 가 작으면 근사 Gaussian이므로 위처럼 parametrize. generation: $x_T\sim\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ → $x_{t-1}\sim p_\theta(x_{t-1}\mid x_t)$ ($t=T,\dots,1$) → $q(x_0)$ 닮은 $x_0$.

**$q(x_0)$ vs $\mathcal{N}(0,I)$.** $q(x_0)$ 는 참 data distribution(복잡·multimodal, closed form 미지, sample만), $\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ 는 단순·고정 reference(sampling/density 자명). $q(x_0)$ 는 직접 sampling 불가, $\mathcal{N}(0,I)$ 는 가능 → forward의 supervision으로 reverse를 학습해 "noise→data" map 실현.
